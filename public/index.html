WWDC 2022 on June 6: iOS 16, macOS 13, what else to expect from the Apple event

HIGHLIGHTS
    At the WWDC 2022 developer event, Apple will showcase its latest software for the iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch and Apple TV.
    Apple will unveil iOS 16, iPadOS 16, macOS 13, tvOS 16 and watchOS 9.
    Apple is expected to release stable updates following the iPhone 14 launch event in September 2022.

WWDC 2022 will be hosted virtually on June 6. The Apple event, which is primarily hosted for the developers' community, will last till June 10, when the iPhone 13 maker will showcase the latest innovations in iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS.
